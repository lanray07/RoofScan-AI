# RoofScan AI Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 17, 2026

RoofScan AI is an iOS app for creating roofing inspections, reviewing roof photos, recording visual findings, and exporting inspection reports.

This policy explains how RoofScan AI handles information when you use the app.

## Information You Provide

RoofScan AI may store the following information on your device:

- Property addresses
- Client names
- Roof inspection notes
- Roof photos you take or upload
- Photo labels and photo notes
- AI-suggested findings
- Manual findings you add
- Generated report summaries
- Locally saved PDF reports
- Business profile and report branding details you enter

This information is used to create and manage roof inspection records and reports.

## Local Storage

RoofScan AI stores inspection data locally on your device using Apple platform storage technologies. Local data remains on your device unless you choose to share or export it.

You can delete local app data from the Settings screen in the app.

## Photos And Camera

RoofScan AI requests access to your camera so you can take roof photos. It requests access to your photo library so you can upload existing photos for inspection reports.

Photos are used only for inspection and report generation features.

## AI Processing

RoofScan AI includes a mock AI mode for development and testing.

If a production remote AI service is enabled, roof photo data and related inspection context may be sent to a secure backend endpoint for processing. The iOS app must not contain third-party AI API keys.

AI findings are visual suggestions only. They are not a certified roof inspection, structural engineering advice, insurance claim approval advice, legal advice, or a safety certification.

## Subscriptions And Purchases

RoofScan AI may offer subscriptions using Apple StoreKit. Purchases and subscription management are handled by Apple. RoofScan AI does not collect or store your full payment card details.

## Sharing And Exports

You may choose to export or share PDF reports from the app. When you share a report, the information in that report is sent to the destination you select, such as email, messaging, cloud storage, or another app.

## Data Collection

The current app build is designed to store inspection data locally on your device and does not include third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.

If analytics, crash reporting, cloud sync, account login, or production AI backend processing are added in the future, this policy and the App Store privacy disclosures should be updated before release.

## Data Retention

Inspection data remains on your device until you delete it, uninstall the app, or remove local data through the app’s settings.

## Children’s Privacy

RoofScan AI is intended for professional and property-related inspection workflows. It is not directed to children.

## Safety Notice

Users should not climb roofs or take unsafe photos. Urgent safety issues should be checked by qualified professionals immediately.
