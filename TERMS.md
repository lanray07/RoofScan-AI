## AI And Inspection Disclaimer

RoofScan AI findings are visual suggestions only.

RoofScan AI is not a replacement for:

- A certified roof inspection
- A structural inspection
- Electrical inspection
- Insurance claim approval advice
- Legal advice

All findings must be reviewed and verified by a qualified professional before use.
